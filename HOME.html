WWDOA is the abbreviation of Women Welfare and Development for Afghanistan.

It is non-profitable unit set-up established to assist in development of Afghanistan and secure the operations of relief and development activities inside Afghanistan through direct cooperation with existing local residents, authorities, shores and donors.





WWDOA was established in 2006 by having full abilities, adequate capacity for executing operational, financial management and implementation qualities started its efforts for development of Afghanistan since 2006 in the sectors of construction, irrigation and income generation which it provide and help womens in any case of living such us languages courses,learning tailory and handcrafts






As an implementing organization (WWDOA) shall be marking itself available to ad ll donor agencies, governmental and nongovernmental, all activities, efforts and required actions including operational, financial and management of (WWDOA) are non-governmental and non-profitable. (WWDOA) has the rights to work any where inside Afghanistan without any restrictions and has full authorization to seek financial resource and implementation possibilities for the planned relief and development projects in Afghanistan. The organization is entitled to receive and accept gifts, grants, aids and donations from any UN and international agency.
